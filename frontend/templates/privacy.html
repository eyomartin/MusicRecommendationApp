Privacy & Data Use

We value your privacy. This page explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you interact with our emotion-based music recommendation system.

What Data We Collect

We only collect the mood or scenario you type into the app to provide accurate recommendations. No personal information is requested or stored.

How We Use Your Data

Your mood input is used in real-time to generate music suggestions. No mood data is stored after you leave the session.

Your Rights

You have full control. You are not required to enter any personal details, and no tracking is performed beyond the current session.
